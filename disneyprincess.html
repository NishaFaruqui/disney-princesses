The Evolution of the Disney Princesses

Disney movies are always a magical experience to watch. They usually have a meaningful impact, giving people hope and a rush of happy feelings. They teach you to always follow your heart and your dreams. While the message is usually quite common, the role that Disney princesses play in conveying such ideas has changed. The first Disney princess films were "Snow White", "Cinderella", and "Sleeping Beauty". These films were released in the 1930's-1950's, and these princesses played the stereotypical role of gentle, young girls who rely on princes to come rescue them. Since then, Disney films have introduced new princesses who are a lot more independent, and empowering, and often defy the social norms-- princesses such as Mulan, Merida, Belle. These princesses are far more heroic and admirable, risking their own lives trying to save the people they love.
